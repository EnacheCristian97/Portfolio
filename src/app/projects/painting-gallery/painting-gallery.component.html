Painting Gallery App

What is this app about

Painting Gallery website is an online platform that showcases artwork from various artists, providing a digital space to browse and purchase paintings. The website can feature a variety of paintings, including contemporary, abstract, landscape, portrait, and still life paintings.
The website is design and focused on creating an immersive experience for the user, with high-quality images of the paintings displayed in a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate layout. Users can browse paintings by artist, style, theme.
